Welcome to Dewey's!

We are a family-friendly, family owned restaurant and bar operating out of the same location for 23 years, offering you the same American favorites and quick bites for all occasions. Dewey’s is famous for our pizzas, burgers, and much more! We would love for you to visit!

Whether you’re a table for one or a group of 25 or more, we have a table ready for you and a fantastic catering crew ready for any occasion, just give us a call or stop on by!
